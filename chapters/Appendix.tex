\section{IV-Conf web pack reflashing}\label{app:reflashing}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Navigate to \url{https://phol-labs.com/iv} and connect to the indicator as described in \autoref{ch:web-config}.
    \item After establishing the MODBUS link, press \texttt{Stop Send Data}.
    \item Click the firmware upload icon, select the firmware file supplied with your indicator, and confirm the action.
    \item Wait for the transfer to complete and the indicator to reboot. Do not disconnect power during reflashing.
\end{enumerate}
